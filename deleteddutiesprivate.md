List of formally or semi-formally delegated duties, responsibilities, or offices by the Council

The following is a list of titles and general responsibilities drawn up by the Council to facilitate the operations of Bombers Bar. As they are titles created by the Council, they may be added, altered, transferred, granted, revoked, or removed by the Council. It is likely that these roles be held almost entirely by members of the Council, but they need not be. These roles also need not be distributed to all different people i.e. an individual may hold multiple titles if they and the Council agree to grant the titles.
C-suite titles:
Chief Event Officer - responsible for planning events and organizing the requisite assets and roles for those events. May also designate individuals to be in charge of or tasked with assorted duties to make those events happen.
	Holder of office: Hellspawn
Chief Asset Officer - responsible for overseeing the assets at top level. Will oversee the cache managers and authorize any larger expenditures to that end e.g. replacing lost blops and rollers in line with Council guidelines.
Holder of office: Lord
Chief Human Resources Officer - responsible for conducting or delegating the task of activity audits of fleet commanders. Responsible for keeping track of all active/inactive/banned/whatever fleet commanders, their ranks as they change, and their BB corp alts. Shall be responsible for granting and revoking roles per current policies set by the Council and layed out in the constitution such that they are current with an individual’s current status within Bombers Bar. Shall coordinate with SFC mentors in regards to their mentees and promotion viability.
	Holder of office: Lord
Chief Financial Officer - responsible for managing the wallets and high level finances. May delegate tasking for any audits or other financial tasks.
Holder of office: Sin




Chief Marketing Officer - responsible for promoting and advertising Bombers Bar across various platforms. May commission assorted bomberganda in general or for various events whether they be hosted by Bombers Bar or an external entity. Responsible for Bombers Bar’s social media presence or the delegation of those duties.
Holder of office: Nova
Chief Logistics Officer - responsible for the movement of assorted Bombers Bar assets. These assets may be ships, items, loot, bombs, fuel, or anything else that the leadership staff require to be moved. Shall coordinate with the cache managers to assist them as needed. May also designate individuals to be in charge of or tasked with assorted duties to accomplish duties.
Holder of office: Chaos

Mundane titles/roles:
Discord Moderator - shall monitor and remove content from the Bombers Bar discord server that violates the discord terms of service. This role is intended to allow an individual that would not ordinarily have the permissions required to remove posts to be granted the power to do so. Such actions shall be recorded through screenshots of the offending content. If the offending content is egregious enough to potentially warrant further action than removal of the content, they shall contact one of the members of the Council or Bombers Bar discord administrators to take further action.
